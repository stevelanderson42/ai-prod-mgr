# Options Trading Approval and Agreement Requirements

## Approval Levels

Options trading privileges are granted in tiered levels based on customer experience, financial profile, and investment objectives:

- **Level 1:** Covered calls and cash-secured puts only. Requires basic options knowledge and a signed Options Agreement.
- **Level 2:** Long calls and puts, spreads, and straddles. Requires demonstrated trading experience and minimum account equity of $10,000.
- **Level 3:** Uncovered (naked) writing. Requires advanced experience, minimum equity of $50,000, and supervisory principal approval.

## Options Agreement

No options order may be entered for a customer account until the customer has signed and returned the Options Agreement and the firm has received and reviewed a completed Options Account Application. The registered representative must document the customer's options experience, annual income, net worth, and stated investment objective before approval is granted.

## Ongoing Review

Accounts approved for Level 2 or above must be reviewed quarterly by the branch manager to confirm continued suitability. Any material change in customer financial circumstances must trigger a reassessment of the approved options level.
