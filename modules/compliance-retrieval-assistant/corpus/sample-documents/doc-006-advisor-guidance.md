# Advisor Communication Guidance (Simplified)

Do:
- Use clear, plain language.
- Present both potential benefits and risks.
- Encourage clients to review disclosures.
- Ask clarifying questions to understand intent.

Do not:
- Guarantee investment performance.
- Represent hypothetical results as typical.
- Imply a product is risk-free.
- Provide personalized recommendations without suitability workflow.

When uncertain:
- Use neutral language.
- Offer to connect the client with an advisor.
- Escalate to compliance for review.
