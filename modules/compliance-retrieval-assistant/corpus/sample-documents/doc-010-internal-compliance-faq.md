# Internal Compliance FAQ (Demo)

Q: Can we say "guaranteed returns"?
A: No. Promissory language is generally prohibited and should be escalated.

Q: Can we share performance information?
A: Yes, but it must be fair and balanced, with disclosures and context.

Q: What if a client insists on certainty?
A: Reiterate that outcomes are not guaranteed; provide standard disclosures.

Q: What if we are unsure how to respond?
A: Use a holding response and route to compliance for review.
