# Client Communication Standards

Requirements:
- Communications must be fair, balanced, and not misleading.
- Avoid promissory or exaggerated claims.
- Avoid "guarantee" language related to performance.

Examples:
Bad: "This investment will guarantee you a 10% return."
Better: "Returns are not guaranteed; performance depends on market conditions."

Tone:
- Professional and neutral.
- Avoid pressure or urgency cues that may be misleading.

Escalation:
If the client requests certainty on outcomes, provide risk disclosures and escalate if needed.
